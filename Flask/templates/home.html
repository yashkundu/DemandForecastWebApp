Food Demand Forecasting

A Food delivery service has to deal with a lot of perishable raw materials which makes it all, the most important factor for such a company is to accurately forcast daily and weekend demand. Too much inventory in the warehouse means more risk of wastage, and not enough could lead to out-of-stocks and push customers to seek solutioins from your competitors. The replenishment of majority of raw materials is done on weekly basis and since the raw material is perishable, the procurement planning is of utmost importance, the task is to predict the demand for the next 10 weeks.
